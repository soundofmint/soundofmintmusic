About
Electro-ethnic live act

Sound of Mint is an electro-ethnic live act by Fadel Lahlou, blending electronic music with rare acoustic instruments from around the world. His performances fuse global traditions with modern rhythms, creating a mystical and energetic soundscape. Supported by icons like Black Coffee, Blond:ish, and Laurent Garnier, Sound of Mint performs worldwide.

Music
PURE by We Love Buzz with Sound of Mint

Sound of Mint, the artistic vision of Fadel Lahlou, unfolds as a deeply personal and evolving musical journey, where electronic textures meet organic instrumentation in a space of freedom and emotion. Rooted in improvisation and shaped by influences from North and Sub Saharan Africa, his sound creates an immersive and intimate sonic experience that transcends cultures globally.
