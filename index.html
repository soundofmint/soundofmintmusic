Bio

Sound of Mint is an electro ethnic live act by Fadel Lahlou, blending electronic music with rare acoustic instruments from around the world.

Supported by icons like Black Coffee, Blondish, and Laurent Garnier, and signed to labels including Sol Selectas and Buddha Bar Records, Sound of Mint has performed over 1000 shows worldwide.
