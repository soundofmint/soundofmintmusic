About
Electro-ethnic live act

Sound of Mint is an electro-ethnic live act by Fadel Lahlou, blending electronic music with rare acoustic instruments from around the world. His performances fuse global traditions with modern rhythms, creating a mystical and energetic soundscape. Supported by icons like Black Coffee, Blond:ish, and Laurent Garnier, and signed on labels such as Sol Selectas, Get Physical Music, Buddha Bar Records, Madorasindahouse, and Café de Anatolia, Sound of Mint has performed over 1000+ shows worldwide, from France to Morocco, to Angola, Greece, Mexico, and the US.

Music
PURE by We Love Buzz with Sound of Mint

Sound of Mint, the artistic vision of Fadel Lahlou, unfolds as a deeply personal and evolving musical journey, where electronic textures meet organic instrumentation in a space of freedom and emotion. Rooted in improvisation and shaped by influences from North and Sub Saharan Africa, his sound creates an immersive and intimate sonic experience that transcends genres across cultures of the world. This performance reflects the essence of his upcoming album WE ARE ONE, set to be released on his newly launched label Tajine Kulture. More than a project, it is a statement of unity, connection, and shared human energy. Through Sound of Mint, Fadel invites listeners into a living dialogue where music becomes a bridge between cultures, moments, and souls. Each live performance whether in a festival, club or intimate setting is conceived as a unique encounter, an invitation to feel, to move, and to reconnect, whether in the heart of the city or in unexpected spaces.
